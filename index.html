Kia ora. I am a designer who builds beautiful and creative products for humans .

  • Instagram

I am interested in ideas that create change.

At the end of 2019, my wife and I decided to move to London, taking an extra four months to get here by traveling the world. Most recently, I have worked with world renowned airline Air New Zealand, leading the design and delivery of the new Inflight Entertainment product over the past year. As a team we have also built workshops to teach design, and design thinking across the company.

Prior to Air New Zealand I helped lead and launch a product called Narrative that enables photographers to tell stories in new and creative ways with their images. I've also designed an award winning interactive editorial about drugs, lies and big pharma. Produced a weird and wacky music video in Joshua Tree for Strahan Coleman. Told the real and gritty stories of young people living with Cancer in New Zealand. Worked on a story exposing the scumbags of the refugee trading pipeline for Huffington post. And posed in a set of purple undies for a Christmas campaign.

I work at the intersection of product and storytelling, drawing on a wide range of interests and hobbies to create new and interesting pathways to design from. I take design seriously, believing it should be kept to a high standard of accountability. You can read some of my thoughts on design here.

Over the past five years I have helped win over 10 international awards including two Gold DINZ Best Awards. I don't believe awards are a great way to understand how someone works, but shinny things are still nice to have.

I am currently living in London, UK with my wife. I'm one of those crazy people who loves running. The best way to my heart is through my stomach (and my liver...). I drink a dangerous amount of caffeine and I also infrequently take photos and post them here. I always love meeting new people.

If you want to get in touch please email me and I'll aim to get back to you as soon as I can.
