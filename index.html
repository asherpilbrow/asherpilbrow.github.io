Kia ora. I am a designer who builds beautiful and creative products for humans*

  • Instagram

  • LinkedIn

I'm a designer of many mediums living in London. I am interested in ideas that change the world. I enjoy working at the intersection of product and storytelling, drawing on a wide range of interests to create new and interesting pathways to design with.

Most recently, I’ve had the opportunity to worked with world renowned airline Air New Zealand, leading the design and delivery of the new Inflight Entertainment product over the past year.

Prior to Air New Zealand I helped lead and launch a product called Narrative that enables photographers to tell stories in new and creative ways with their images. I've also designed an award winning interactive editorial about drugs, lies and big pharma. Produced a weird and wacky music video in Joshua Tree for Strahan Coleman. Told the real and gritty stories of young people living with Cancer in New Zealand. Worked on a story exposing the scumbags of the refugee trading pipeline for Huffington post. And modelled in a set of purple undies for a Christmas campaign (all evidence has been destroyed).

I work at the intersection of product and storytelling, drawing on a wide range of interests and hobbies to create new and interesting pathways to design from. I take design seriously, believing it should be kept to a high standard of accountability. You can read some of my thoughts on design here.

Over the past five years I have helped win over 10 international awards including two Gold DINZ Best Awards. I don't believe awards are a great way to understand how someone works, but shinny things are still nice to have.

At the end of 2019, my wife and I decided to move to London, taking an extra four months to get here by traveling the world. We've recently arrived, and are getting settled into to a very abnormal London life.

I am currently living in London, UK with my wife. I'm one of those crazy people who loves running. The best way to my heart is through my stomach. I drink a dangerous amount of caffeine and I also infrequently take photos. You can read some of my thoughts on design here.

I always love meeting new people. If you want to get in touch please email me and I'll aim to get back to you as soon as I can.
